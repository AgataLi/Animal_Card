Tibetan Mastiff

It is one of the oldest dog breeds. 12 dog breeds studied appeared to have diverged from the gray wolf 42,000 years ago, the Tibetan Mastiff lineage diverged earlier at 58,000 years ago.

  • Name: Drog-Khyi (tyb.) nomad dog
  • Height at the withers: 61-76cm
  • Weight: 34-76kg
  • Average Lifespan: 12-15 years
  • Temperance: Stubborn, Intelligent, Calm, Caring
  • Color: Black, Brown, Tan, Blue Gray, Red Gold

Originating with the nomadic cultures of Tibet, China, India, Mongolia and Nepal, it is used by local tribes of Tibetans to protect sheep from wolves, leopards, bears, large mustelids, and tigers. Today they are one of the most expansive dogs - in 2011 in China one puppy have been sold for 10 million yuan, which is more than million US Dolar.
